Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Nutrilogix

How accurate is the AI food recognition?

Our AI food recognition system has been trained on millions of food images and can identify thousands of different foods and dishes. It has an accuracy rate of over 90% for common foods. For complex dishes, it breaks down components and provides detailed nutritional information for each ingredient. We're continuously improving the model with user feedback.

Is Nutrilogix available on both iOS and Android?

Yes! Nutrilogix will be available for both iOS and Android devices when we launch. We're currently developing and testing both versions to ensure a seamless experience regardless of your device preference.

Can I track meals eaten at restaurants?

Absolutely! One of Nutrilogix's key features is the ability to analyze restaurant meals. Simply take a photo of your meal, and our AI will identify the food and provide accurate nutritional estimates based on portion size. For chain restaurants, we also have a database of menu items for even more precise tracking.

How do I set personalized nutrition goals?

When you first set up your Nutrilogix account, you'll complete a brief questionnaire about your age, weight, height, activity level, and goals (weight loss, maintenance, muscle gain, etc.). Based on this information, we'll calculate recommended daily targets for calories and macronutrients. You can further customize these targets in the app settings at any time.

Is there a subscription fee?

Nutrilogix will offer both free and premium subscription options. The free version includes basic food tracking and nutritional information. The premium subscription unlocks advanced features like meal planning, detailed nutritional insights, progress analytics, and unlimited food recognition. Early access users will receive a special discount on premium subscriptions.

How does the barcode scanning feature work?

Our barcode scanning feature allows you to quickly log packaged foods. Simply use your phone's camera to scan the barcode, and Nutrilogix will retrieve the nutritional information from our extensive database of over 1 million products. This feature works offline too, so you can scan products even without an internet connection.
